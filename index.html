Hackspace

You have a great idea or just want to hack but don't find the time, people or motivation to start coding? Hackspace is an event where people get together just to enjoy coding. Sometimes something useful comes out, but that is not the reason we are doing it. It is all about exploring latest technologies and having fun learning while doing.

Social

We have regular meetings and get-togethers each month where we discuss exciting ideas while drinking a beer (or two), traveling to CS conferences, visiting companies or beating each other at video games, etc.

Lab

Computer Science is a fast evolving field. Sometimes it is hard to keep track of its development. Even recent breakthroughs go unnoticed. ACM Lab is a repository for CS enthusiasts from CS enthusiasts to form study groups with special interests.
